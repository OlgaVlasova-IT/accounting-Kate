Services

Full range of accounting services from basic slip returns to rental properties, sole proprietorships, small corporate businesses, and GST returns.

Personal Tax Return Bookkeeping Payrolls Audit Support
Filing your personal income tax returns can be confusing and overwhelming, and this is understandable. By engaging our trusted personal income tax service, you will be maximizing your tax savings without the hassles of researching tax planning strategies and the various deductions and credits applicable to your situation.

This is for you if you are ready to take the bookkeeping and accounting off your plate, want clear financial insights into how the business is doing, and want the peace of mind that your business finances are taken care of.
If you need to collect and submit sales taxes, we can assist with ensuring you are charging and collecting the right amount of sales tax. We will file your PST and GST/HST returns, and ensure you are claiming all the input tax credits you are entitled to
We can connect you with a third-party payroll provider, or work with your current vendor. We oversee the payroll process, ensuring all remittances are taken care of.
An audit is nothing to be scared of! We can take care of it hassle free with our streamlined process. We will explain the audit and collect the necessary documents, organize them and submit it. We will even speak to CRA on your behalf and keep track of the progress until the audit is approved.
